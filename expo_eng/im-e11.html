Graphic crossing



  • 5 photos (120 x 100 cm each)
  • and 5 light boxes of 40 x 60 cm each
  • BECH Gallery
  • Santiago, Chile
  • May 2004

Fund for the Development of Culture and Arts, FONDART, 2003.


The FONDART project -Five Graphic and Photographic Interventions- consisted of the production of five minimal graphic interventions in Santiago Centro, their registration in digital format, and the subsequent intervention and manipulation of these images. The project was based on the possibility of digitally editing the gap between the "done" and the "desired", between the first impulse and the retrospective action, between the adaptation to the circumstances and a fiction about the production of work.

- Culture Square, Estación Mapocho, Santiago, Chile (8 de septiembre 2003)
- Museum of Contemporary Art of Santiago, Chile (15 de septiembre 2003)
- La Moneda Palace, Santiago, Chile (19 de octubre 2003)
- Plaza Italia, Santiago, Chile (2 de noviembre 2003)
- Museum of Natural History, Quinta Normal, frontis Museo de Historia Natural, Santiago, Chile (24 de noviembre 2003
