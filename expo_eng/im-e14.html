30 vector drawings
This set of digital images were made up of vector drawings of portraits of 24 files from the Archives of Terror in Paraguay, recorded during a visit to the Documentation and Archive Center at the Palace of Justice in Asuncion, in July 2006, in the context of an art residency at the Museo del Barro. They were accompanied by 3 diagrams of the cards and 3 images generated by a program that detects the movements of the digital pencil when drawing on the computer the images mentioned above.
